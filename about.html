About Bohol

Bohol is a beautiful island province in the Philippines, known for its Chocolate Hills, white-sand beaches, and the tiny tarsier. It offers great diving spots like Panglao Island and rich history with landmarks like the Blood Compact Shrine and Baclayon Church. With stunning nature, friendly locals, and delicious food, Bohol is a perfect place for adventure and relaxation.
