Municipalities of Bohol

Bohol consists of various municipalities, each with its own rich history, culture, and attractions.

Tagbilaran

The capital and commercial center of Bohol, serving as the main gateway to the province.

Panglao

Famous for its white-sand beaches, world-class diving spots, and luxury resorts.

Loboc

Known for the Loboc River Cruise, the Loboc Children's Choir, and adventure activities.

Carmen

Home to the iconic Chocolate Hills, one of Bohol's top attractions.

Dauis

Features historical churches, stunning ocean views, and is close to Panglao.

Anda

A rising beach destination with pristine white beaches, caves, and snorkeling spots.
